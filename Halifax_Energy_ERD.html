3NF Compliance: All non-key attributes depend solely on the primary key of their entity (no transitive dependencies). Dim_Date eliminates temporal repetition from Fact_Energy_Weather — Hour, DayOfWeek, Month, Is_Holiday are stored once per timestamp. Geo_LandUse eliminates zone attribute repetition from Geo_EnergyByZone. Staging tables are excluded from 3NF scope per convention (raw ingest layer, not analytics layer). ETL_Watermark is a control table with no FK dependencies by design.
